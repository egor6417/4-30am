FASHION @ 4:30am

Hermes

Hermes did what it does best- beautiful, somewhat seasonal colors (think a lot of maroon), and simple silhouettes. I don't understand why a leather house feels the need to do ready-to-wear, as it looks like a better-quality Uniqlo. The show was wonderful, the models walking in between two fires. It would've been a real pity if the wonderful alligator coat caught fire, as I loved that thing!
